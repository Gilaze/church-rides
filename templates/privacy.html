Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you register:

  • Full name
  • Username
  • Grade level
  • Residence location
  • Password (encrypted)
How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for:

  • Coordinating transportation for church events
  • Matching passengers with drivers
  • Internal church administration
Data Security

We protect your information by:

  • Encrypting all passwords
  • Using secure HTTPS connections
  • Storing data on secure servers
  • Limiting access to authorized administrators only
Data Sharing

We do NOT share your information with third parties. Your data is only visible to:

  • Church administrators
  • Drivers you're assigned to (name and grade only)
Your Rights

You can:

  • Update your information in your profile
  • Request deletion of your account by contacting an administrator
  • View what data we have about you
Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact a church administrator.
